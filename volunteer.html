VOLUNTEER

Are you interested in volunteering at Cancer Treatment Centers of America® at Western Regional Medical Center?

 The H.O.P.E. Team provides the volunteer program at Western Regional Medical Center.  These volunteers are a vital part of the team that provides hope, healing, and care to patients and families affected by cancer.

If you are looking for a life-changing experience and are available 3-4 hours a week, then the H.O.P.E. Team volunteer team may be just what you are seeking.

 A program information and orientation session is typically offered the first Friday of every month. After you’ve applied, you will receive further information about upcoming orientations.
