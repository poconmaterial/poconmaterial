Through the H.O.P.E. Team's Wish List, the community is able to donate needed items to support the patients at CTCA. For ideas on how to hold a Wish List drive with your group, contact the H.O.P.E. Team Manager at 623-207-3545.

100% of financial donations to the H.O.P.E. Team go directly to providing activities and supplies for the patients at CTCA and are tax deductible. The H.O.P.E. Team is a 501c3 nonprofit organization (Tax ID#27-0325096).
